The LCL Awards Inclusivity Charter helps approved centres take active steps to make their training facilities welcoming, flexible and supportive for learners and centre staff. It provides a framework for inclusive practice across the approved-centre network, helping more people access opportunities in building services engineering.

LCL Awards — common questions

LCL Awards is a specialist awarding organisation and certification body for the Building Services Engineering sector. It offers qualifications and certification services through a nationwide network of approved centres.

LCL Awards covers gas, electrical, plumbing, heating, renewables, oil, smart metering, solid fuel and biomass, refrigeration and F-Gas, heating design, and gas safety management and auditing.

Use the approved-centre search to find centres offering LCL Awards qualifications in your area.

Visit the Centre Zone to learn about approval requirements, support, standards of service and the online application process.

LCL Awards is recognised as an awarding organisation by qualification regulators including Ofqual, Qualifications Wales and CCEA Regulation, and is also a UKAS accredited certification body.

The Inclusivity Charter helps approved centres create welcoming, inclusive and supportive training environments for learners and staff.
